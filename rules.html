How it works
⏱️
Time window
Professor Rejeb opens a MoM question with a set time limit (e.g. 10 minutes). Solve it, then come back here and submit.
💡
Try on your own first
There may be no winner yet — or time may run out before you can use their help. Don't wait. Your parameters are unique to you anyway.
🥇
First to finish = Race Winner
The first student to submit goes to the board and walks the class through their solution. The winner may also post their MATLAB code live on screen to help others finish.
🗳️
Vote after the race
Once you submit your MoM answer, you have 5 minutes to vote on the winner's solution helpfulness. All participants vote — results are public.

Voting & Eligibility
🗳️ Voting rules
  • Voting opens the moment you submit your MoM answer
  • You have exactly 5 minutes to cast your vote
  • Only participants who submitted can vote
  • Results are shown publicly on the leaderboard
🏅 Who can run next race?
  • ❌ Two consecutive wins → must sit out once
  • ❌ Approval rating < 60% → must sit out next race
  • ✅ All others are eligible to run
  • Eligibility is posted before each race opens
  • Professor Rejeb has final say on all overrides
Winner's solution disclaimer
⚠️
Not reviewed by Professor Rejeb
The winner's solution is shared for learning purposes only. It has not been reviewed or verified by Professor Rejeb. Grading is done solely by MoM — and your parameter values are randomized and unique to you. Do not copy values — use it as a method guide only.
Academic integrity
⚠️
AI Tools & Cheating — Fair Play

Using AI tools, copying another student's solution, or submitting without genuinely working through the problem defeats the purpose of this activity and is unfair to your classmates.

Consequence: forfeiture of bonus points for that race and potential loss of eligibility for future races.

Remember — MoM grades your answer with your own unique randomized values. The winner's MATLAB code will not give you the right answers — your parameters are different. Trying on your own is the only way to actually earn the points.
